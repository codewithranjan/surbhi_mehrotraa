Urban Informatics Lab (UIL)

The Urban Informatics Lab (UIL) at Maulana Azad National Institute of Technology (MANIT), Bhopal, specializes in the research and analysis of urban contexts with a niche in Environmental Planning. Founded and led by Dr. Surabhi Mehrotra, UIL focuses on urban morphology, sustainable planning, and environmental conservation through rigorous research, data collection, and urban environment studies. The lab also provides comprehensive training for students in geographical information systems (GIS), data analysis, and data interpretation, equipping them with the necessary skills to excel in urban and environmental planning fields.
